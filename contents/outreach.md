#### Deutscher Studienpreis Essay (German only)

<a href="/static/assets/Leuchtende Mikroplastikpartikel Essay Vera Titze.pdf" target="_blank">Here</a>, you can find my essay contributed to the Deutscher Studienpreis 2024 about my Ph.D. research. In this essay, I share the story behind our collaborative project of turning microplastic particles into small lasers and using those to monitor microplastic transport in live corals.
It is written for a general, scientifically interested audience.
